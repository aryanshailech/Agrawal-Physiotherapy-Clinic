Our History

Agrawal Physiotherapy Clinic was established in 2005 with a mission to provide high-quality physiotherapy services to our community. Our founder, Dr. Rajesh Agrawal, envisioned a place where patients could receive personalized care tailored to their unique needs. Starting with a small team of dedicated professionals, the clinic quickly grew in reputation due to its effective treatment methods and patient-centric approach.

Over the years, the clinic has expanded its services to include advanced rehabilitation programs, sports physiotherapy, and pediatric care. We have invested in state-of-the-art equipment and continuous staff training to ensure we remain at the forefront of physiotherapy practice. Today, Agrawal Physiotherapy Clinic is recognized as a leading provider of physiotherapy services in the region, known for our commitment to improving patient outcomes and quality of life.

Founder

Dr. Rajesh Agrawal

Dr. Rajesh Agrawal, a highly experienced physiotherapist, founded the clinic with a vision to offer personalized and effective treatment to patients. With over 20 years of experience in the field, Dr. Agrawal has been instrumental in the clinic's success.

Dr. Agrawal graduated from XYZ University with a degree in Physiotherapy and further specialized in orthopedic physiotherapy. He has been actively involved in research and has published numerous papers on innovative physiotherapy techniques. His dedication to patient care and continuous learning has earned him respect and recognition within the medical community.

Under his leadership, Agrawal Physiotherapy Clinic has received multiple awards and accolades, solidifying its reputation as a trusted healthcare provider. Dr. Agrawal continues to inspire his team with his passion for excellence and unwavering commitment to patient well-being.

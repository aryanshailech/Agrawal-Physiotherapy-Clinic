Meet Our Experts

Our team of highly skilled and experienced physiotherapists is dedicated to providing the best care and support to help you achieve your health and wellness goals. Get to know our team members below.

Dr. Ankit Agrawal

Founder & Chief Physiotherapist

Dr. Ankit Agrawal has over 20 years of experience in the field of physiotherapy. He specializes in sports injuries, orthopaedic conditions, and neurological rehabilitation. Dr. Agrawal is committed to providing personalized care and developing innovative treatment plans for his patients.

He completed his PhD in Physiotherapy from XYZ University and has been a guest speaker at numerous international conferences. Dr. Agrawal's research has been published in several prestigious medical journals, and he is passionate about advancing the field of physiotherapy through evidence-based practice.

Outside of his professional life, Dr. Agrawal enjoys marathon running and is an avid supporter of community health initiatives.

Dr. Priya Sharma

Senior Physiotherapist

With a strong background in geriatric physiotherapy, Dr. Priya Sharma has been helping older adults maintain their independence and improve their quality of life for over 15 years. Her compassionate approach and expertise in balance training make her a valuable asset to our team.

Dr. Sharma earned her Master's degree in Geriatric Physiotherapy from ABC University and has since dedicated her career to improving elderly care. She has developed specialized programs for fall prevention and mobility enhancement, which have greatly benefited her patients.

In her free time, Dr. Sharma volunteers at local senior centers and enjoys gardening.

Dr. Rajesh Patel

Paediatric Physiotherapist

Dr. Rajesh Patel specializes in pediatric physiotherapy, focusing on diagnosing, preventing, and treating developmental disorders in children. His friendly demeanor and expertise in play-based therapy help children achieve their developmental milestones.

Dr. Patel holds a Doctorate in Pediatric Physiotherapy from DEF University and is a certified specialist in treating conditions such as cerebral palsy and congenital disabilities. He is known for his innovative therapy techniques that incorporate play and creativity to engage young patients.

A father of two, Dr. Patel enjoys spending time with his family and is actively involved in youth sports programs in the community.
